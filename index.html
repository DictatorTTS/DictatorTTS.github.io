What is Dictator?

Dictator is a lightweight, always-on-top microphone overlay for Linux desktops. Press a hotkey, speak, and your words are typed anywhere — any application, any text field. All transcription happens locally using OpenAI Whisper — nothing ever leaves your machine.

⬇ Download on GitHub

Features

  • Instant one-key push-to-talk overlay
  • Local Whisper AI transcription — no internet required
  • Vocabulary editor — teach it words and phrases it mishears
  • Draggable, always-on-top 60×60 px overlay widget
  • Dark-themed settings panel
  • Punctuation control (speak or strip automatically)
  • Scales correctly at any DPI / display scaling

Privacy

Dictator processes all audio locally. No audio, text, or usage data is ever transmitted to any server. Your words stay on your machine.
